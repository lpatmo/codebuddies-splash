Weekly CodeBuddies Community Updates

Productivity is sometimes hard.

Share a weekly update with a supportive community! Your responses will go out in a weekly newsletter to the CodeBuddies community. This is like a big mastermind group -- except all updates will be anonymous.

Examples:

  • a link to a project or blog post you worked on
  • a reflection about a thing you learned this week
  • a shout-out about a friend's project
  • a question you have for the community
      • All submitted entries will be published anonymously unless you out yourself in the message.
      • You can submit as many times in a week as you want.
      • An archive of weekly updates will be posted here.
