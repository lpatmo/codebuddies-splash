Weekly CodeBuddies Community Updates

Productivity is sometimes hard.

Share a weekly update with a supportive community! Your responses will go out in a weekly newsletter to the CodeBuddies community. This is like a big mastermind group -- except all updates will be anonymous unless you out yourself in the message.

Examples of what you could share:

  • something small you learned about CSS or Javascript
  • a link to a project you're starting or a blog post you published
  • a shout-out to a friend's project
  • a question you have for the community
      • You can submit as many entries in a week as you want.
      • An archive of weekly updates will be posted here.
