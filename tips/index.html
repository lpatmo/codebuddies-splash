Hangout Best Practices

Important

  1. Turn off your video camera to save on bandwidth.
  2. Screen share by clicking on the green icon on the left (and the first window that appears after that). If everyone shares their screens, we can help each other more easily, see what we've each coded, and also be able to point out what we're talking about more easily.
  3. Take turns talking through new concepts and the code out loud. Give everyone a chance to speak and ask questions. A good number for an efficient study session, in fact, is between 2-5 people -- all working on the same exercises.
